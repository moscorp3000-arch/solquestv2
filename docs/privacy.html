Privacy Policy — SolQuest

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 8, 2026

Short version: SolQuest collects zero personal data. We do not track you, store your information, or sell anything. Your wallet stays yours.

1. Who We Are & What SolQuest Is

SolQuest is a quiz-based learning app for the Solana Mobile Seeker device, built by SeekerLabs. Users test their knowledge across 10 modules with 71 shuffled questions about Solana, DeFi, NFTs, and Web3. A small verification transaction (~0.000001 SOL) confirms module completion on-chain.

Data controller: SeekerLabs (Rotterdam, The Netherlands)
Contact: eds3000@proton.me

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

SolQuest does not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Private keys or seed phrases — ever

All module progress, XP, and streak data is stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage and never leaves your device unless you complete an on-chain verification transaction.

3. Blockchain & Wallet Data

SolQuest interacts with Solana mainnet. Blockchain transactions are public by nature. When you complete a module or verify your wallet:

  • Your public wallet address is associated with the completion
  • A small transaction is recorded on-chain
  • All signing happens through the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter. We never see your private keys or seed phrase.

4. Third-Party Services

SolQuest uses the following third parties — each receives only your public wallet address:

  • Helius RPC — Solana node provider for reading/writing mainnet state
  • Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter — for wallet connection
  • Metaplex — compressed NFT badge minting (optional)

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679):

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — request a copy of any data we hold
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — request deletion
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — receive your data in machine-readable form
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — object to processing
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18) — restrict how your data is processed
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (for NL: Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens)

Because SolQuest does not collect personal data server-side, most of these rights are fulfilled automatically. To exercise any right, email eds3000@proton.me.

6. Data Retention

We retain no personal data on our servers. All SolQuest data is either:

  • Local only (module progress, XP, streak) — deleted when you uninstall the app
  • On-chain (verification transactions, NFT badges) — permanent by design of the Solana blockchain; we cannot delete on-chain records

7. Legal Basis for Processing

Where any processing of personal data does occur (e.g. your public wallet address when signing a transaction), the legal basis under GDPR Art. 6(1) is:

  • (b) Performance of a contract — to deliver the SolQuest service you have requested (module verification, airdrop eligibility)

8. Children's Privacy

SolQuest is not directed at children under 16 (EEA) or 13 (US). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used SolQuest, contact eds3000@proton.me.

9. Contact & Changes

Questions, complaints, or GDPR requests: eds3000@proton.me

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be announced in the app. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
