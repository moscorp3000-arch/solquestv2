Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 2, 2026

Short version: SeekerLabs collects zero personal data. We do not track you, store your information, or sell anything. Your wallet stays yours.

1. Who We Are

SeekerLabs builds mobile apps for the Solana Mobile ecosystem. Our apps — SeekerQuest and SolQuest — run on Solana Mobile Seeker devices.

Contact: eds3000@proton.me

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Private keys or seed phrases — ever

3. Blockchain Interactions

Our apps interact with Solana mainnet. Transactions are public by nature. When you complete a quest or earn a badge, a transaction is recorded on-chain. This is how the Solana network works.

All signing happens through Seed Vault — Solana Mobile's secure hardware enclave. We never see your private keys.

4. Wallet Permissions

We only request:

  • Sign transactions — to verify quest completion on-chain
  • Public wallet address — to associate badges with your wallet

5. Third-Party Services

  • Helius RPC — Solana node provider
  • Jupiter Aggregator — token swap routing
  • Metaplex — compressed NFT badge minting

These services only receive your public wallet address.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Questions? Email: eds3000@proton.me
